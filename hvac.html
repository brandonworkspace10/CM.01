Yes. It identifies urgent situations — no cooling, no heat, system not responding — and immediately texts your on-call technician with the customer's name, number, address, and issue description. Emergency routing is configured during onboarding to match your escalation protocol.
Can it handle the summer surge in call volume?
Calling Matrix never gets overloaded. Whether you get 10 calls or 300 during a heat wave, every caller is answered within 2 seconds. There's no staffing up, no overflow, and no missed calls regardless of volume.
