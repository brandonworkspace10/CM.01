FAQ

Questions roofing contractors
ask us every day.

Can it really handle a storm surge of 300+ calls?
Yes. There's no capacity limit. Every call is answered within 2 seconds regardless of simultaneous volume — whether it's a quiet Tuesday or the night after a major hailstorm.
Does it know about insurance claims?
It's trained to identify insurance claim scenarios and can collect carrier name, policy number, and claim details as part of the intake. It can also explain your company's claim-assist process to homeowners.
Can it book estimate appointments?
Yes. It books estimates directly into your calendar with address, damage description, insurance status, and preferred time all captured upfront — so every estimate appointment is fully qualified.
What if we get calls from outside our service area?
It screens by zip code and service area before confirming an estimate, so your team only goes out to qualified leads in your territory.
Does it follow up with leads that don't book right away?
Yes. Automated follow-up sequences contact every lead that didn't convert, at intervals you set during onboarding — turning cold leads into booked estimates.
