FAQ

Questions plumbing owners
ask us every day.

Can it identify and route flooding emergencies?
Yes. It listens for urgency indicators — active flooding, water heater failure, sewage backup — and immediately texts your emergency line with the caller's name, address, and situation.
What if a caller is outside our service area?
It politely explains your service territory and declines to book. You can also have it take their information in case your coverage area expands — no caller leaves empty-handed.
Can it answer questions about our pricing?
Yes. We load your standard rates and service call fees during onboarding so callers get accurate pricing information without tying up your dispatcher.
Does it integrate with ServiceTitan?
Yes. Bookings sync directly to ServiceTitan, Jobber, and Housecall Pro. Jobs appear on your techs' schedules automatically with full customer details.
How long does setup take?
Most plumbing companies are fully live within 48 hours after a 30-minute onboarding call. We capture your services, territory, pricing, and emergency protocols — then build and deploy.
