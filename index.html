07 / Questions

Common questions from busy owners.

Does it actually sound human?
Yes — and callers almost never realize it's AI. Calling Matrix interrupts naturally, handles accents and background noise, and follows the pacing of a trained dispatcher. Hear a real recording on your demo call.
Will it work with my existing CRM?
Native, two-way integrations with Housecall Pro, ServiceTitan, Jobber, and Google Calendar. Also connects via Zapier and webhooks to anything else you run. Keep your existing stack.
How long does it take to get set up?
Most shops are live in under 48 hours. Day 1 is a 30-minute onboarding call. Day 2 your number is live and your calendar is synced. Booking jobs that same afternoon is the norm.
What happens if the AI can't answer something?
It either transfers to an on-call human you specify, or it captures the full context and sends you a text so you can call back in seconds — never 24 hours later.
Can it handle after-hours emergencies?
Yes. You define what counts as an emergency (burst pipe, no heat, electrical hazard). Emergencies route straight to your on-call tech. Non-urgent calls get booked for next-available.
Is there a long-term contract?
No. Month-to-month on every plan. 30-day money-back guarantee. If it doesn't pay for itself in more bookings, you don't pay.
