FAQ

Questions electrical contractors
ask us every day.

Can it differentiate commercial and residential calls?
Yes. It asks qualifying questions and routes accordingly — residential bookings go to your scheduling queue, commercial leads get priority handling and expedited follow-up from your sales team.
What happens with electrical safety emergencies?
Calls with safety indicators — sparks, burning smell, complete power loss, exposed wiring — are routed immediately to your emergency line. Safety situations are never put in a queue.
Can it capture details for estimate calls?
Absolutely. It collects service address, job description, square footage if needed, and preferred appointment windows before confirming the estimate — so you arrive prepared every time.
Does it integrate with Jobber?
Yes. Bookings and estimate appointments sync directly to Jobber, ServiceTitan, and Housecall Pro with full client details automatically populated.
Can we customize what questions it asks?
Yes. During onboarding we tailor the intake script to your specific services, licensing requirements, job minimums, and any qualification questions your team normally asks on first contact.
